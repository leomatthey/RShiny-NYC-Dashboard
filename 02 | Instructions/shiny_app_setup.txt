Notes to upload app to AWS instance and correct setup:

Copy your app folder from your local machine to AWS machine. Using your local console:
scp -i "your_PEM_file.pem" -r your_path_to_app ubuntu@Public IPv4 DNS:/home/ubuntu/

Then copy to the final destination (/srv/shiny-server/). Using AWS machine console (as ubuntu user):
sudo cp -r ~/app_folder /srv/shiny-server/

Change permissions to read/edit/execute
sudo chmod -R 777 /srv/shiny-server/app_folder/
Change paths inside .R files for a correct execution using:
vi your_app_file.R (look at vi manual to learn how to)

Try to reach your app from an internet browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc)
Public_IPv4_address:3838/app_folder/

If everything is right, you will see your app.

If something goes wrong, look at the log files at /var/log/shiny-server
sudo cat /var/log/shiny-server/your_app_logfile.log

Fix errors, and try to reach again.
